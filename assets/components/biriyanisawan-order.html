About Our Pot Biryani

Our authentic pot biryani is prepared using traditional dum cooking method, where marinated meat and partially cooked rice are layered in a handi (pot) and slow-cooked to perfection. This method allows the flavors to infuse deeply into every grain of rice.

Chicken Biryani:

Tender chicken pieces marinated in yogurt and spices, layered with fragrant basmati rice and cooked with saffron and caramelized onions.

Chicken & Egg Biryani:

Our special combination of chicken and boiled eggs, offering double the protein and flavor in every bite.

Egg Biryani:

Perfect for vegetarians who enjoy eggs, featuring perfectly boiled eggs with the same rich biryani flavors.

Seafood Varieties:

Choose between succulent prawns or flaky fish pieces cooked with coastal spices for a unique taste experience.

Vegetable Biryani:

A flavorful vegetarian option with seasonal vegetables, nuts, and the same aromatic spices as our meat varieties.

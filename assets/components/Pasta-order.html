About Our Pasta

Our pasta is made fresh daily using traditional Italian methods and premium ingredients. We offer a variety of sauces and toppings to satisfy every palate. All our pasta dishes are cooked al dente for the perfect texture.

Chicken Pasta:

Tender chicken pieces sautéed with garlic and herbs, combined with your choice of sauce and pasta.

Egg Pasta:

Fresh eggs cooked perfectly and combined with our signature sauces for a protein-packed meal.

Prawns Pasta:

Succulent prawns cooked with garlic, chili flakes, and white wine for a delightful seafood experience.

Sea Food Pasta:

A mix of fresh seafood including prawns, mussels, and calamari in a rich tomato or white wine sauce.

Mushroom Pasta:

Seasonal mushrooms sautéed with garlic and herbs in a creamy or tomato-based sauce.

Cheese Pasta:

Our famous cheese-loaded pasta with customizable cheese levels and types.

Vegetable Pasta:

Fresh seasonal vegetables stir-fried with garlic and combined with your favorite sauce.

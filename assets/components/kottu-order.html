About Our Kottu

Kottu is Sri Lanka's most popular street food, made by chopping and stir-frying flatbread (roti) with vegetables, eggs, meat, and a blend of aromatic spices. Our kottu is prepared fresh to order with authentic flavors.

Regular Kottu:

The classic version made with chopped godamba roti, vegetables, eggs, and your choice of protein.

Dolphin Kottu:

A special version where the roti is cut into thin strips resembling dolphin fins, with extra egg and vegetables.

String Hopper Kottu:

Made with chopped string hoppers instead of roti, for a lighter texture and unique flavor.

Mensa Special Kottu:

Our signature kottu with a secret blend of spices, extra vegetables, and premium cuts of meat.
